Vanilla Cupcakes

Ingredients

2 teaspoons of baking powder
½ teaspoon of baking soda
2 tablespoons of cornstarch
1 7/8 cups of flour 3/8 cups of granulated sugar
¼ teaspoon of salt
2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
½ cup of vegetable oil
1 1/3 cups of milk

Recipe

Preheat your oven to 190°C
In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cornstarch, sugar, and salt.
In another bowl, mix together the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract until well combined.
Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the wet mixture.
Stir until just combined. Do not overmix; the batter should be slightly lumpy.
Divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups, filling each about 2/3 full.
Bake in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
The muffins should be golden brown on top.
Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
Enjoy your vanilla cupcakes!
