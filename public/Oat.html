Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients

2 bananas, large ripe
2 cups rolled oats
½ cup almond butter
3 tbsp maple syrup
½ tbsp cinnamon
¼ tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/3 cup walnuts

Recipe

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
In a mixing bowl, mash the bananas until smooth.
Add the rolled oats, almond butter, maple syrup, cinnamon, salt, and vanilla extract to the mashed bananas. Stir until well combined.
Fold in the chopped walnuts.
Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
Flatten each cookie slightly with the back of a spoon or your fingers.
Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the cookies are golden brown around the edges.
Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
Once cooled, store the oatmeal cookies in an airtight container. Enjoy your delicious and healthy treats!
