Scones

Ingredients

3 cups of flour
2 tbsp of baking powder
3 tbsp of sugar
¼ cup of butter
1 cup of milk

Recipe


Preheat your oven to 220°C. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and sugar.
Add the cold cubed butter to the flour mixture. Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Gradually pour in the milk, stirring until the dough just comes together. Be careful not to overmix.
Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it holds together.
Pat the dough into a circle that's about 1 inch thick. Use a sharp knife to cut the circle into 8 wedges.
Transfer the wedges to the prepared baking sheet, leaving a little space between each one.
Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the scones are lightly golden brown on top.
Remove from the oven and let the scones cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
Serve the scones warm with butter, jam, or clotted cream, if desired.
Enjoy your delicious scones!
