Cottage Cheese Banana Bread

Recipe Page

Ingredients

2 bananas, ripe
3 eggs
3 tablespoons of maple syrup
2 ½ cups of almond flour
1 teaspoon of baking soda 2 teaspoons of cinnamon
2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
½ cup of cottage cheese

Recipe

Preheat your oven to 175°C
In a large mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas until smooth. Add the eggs, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and cottage cheese. Mix well until all ingredients are combined.
In another bowl, whisk together the almond flour, baking soda, and cinnamon.
Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can make the bread dense.
Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, spreading it out evenly.
Bake in the preheated oven for about 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. The baking time can vary, so start checking around the 50-minute mark. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
Enjoy your cottage cheese banana bread!
