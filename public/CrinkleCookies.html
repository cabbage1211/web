Lemon Crinkle Cookies

Ingredients

1 teaspoon of lemon zest
1 egg, large
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 cups of all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon of baking soda
¾ cup of powdered sugar
¼ teaspoon of salt
1 ¼ cup of sugar
½ teaspoon of vanilla extract
½ cup of butter

Recipe

Preheat your oven to 175°C
In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
In a large bowl, using an electric mixer, beat the softened butter and 1 ¼ cup of sugar together until light and fluffy.
In a large bowl, using an electric mixer, beat the softened butter and 1 ¼ cup of sugar together until light and fluffy.
Beat in the egg, lemon zest, lemon juice, and vanilla extract until well combined.
Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Do not overmix.
Cover the dough and refrigerate for about 30 minutes. This makes it easier to handle and helps the cookies maintain their shape.
Place the powdered sugar in a small bowl. Scoop tablespoon-sized balls of dough and roll them into balls. Roll each ball in the powdered sugar until well coated.
Place the coated dough balls onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set and the tops are cracked.
Enjoy your crinkle lemon cookies!
