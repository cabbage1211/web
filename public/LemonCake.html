Lemon Poppyseed Cake

Ingredients

1 tbsp lemon
3 eggs
1 ½ cup of all-purpose flour
2 tsp of baking powder
1 pinch salt
2 tbsp of poppy seeds
¾ cup of brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
½ cup of cooking oil
½ cup of yoghurt

Recipe

Preheat your oven to 175°C. Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.
In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, brown sugar, vanilla extract, lemon zest, cooking oil, and yogurt until well combined.
In another bowl, sift together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.
Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
Gently fold in the poppy seeds until evenly distributed throughout the batter.
Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing and serving.
Slice and enjoy your delicious lemon poppy seed cake!
